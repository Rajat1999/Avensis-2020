Sike the Psych

Description

Sike the Psych is a one of a kind event where the participants are supposed to apply their brains to think out of the box and challenge conventional thinking.
Round 1- Follow the Tale
Participants will be shown 4 pictures.
They have to explain the situation shown and keep connecting the situations together as the pictures are shown.
Round 2- Defend the Devil
Participants will be given a statement, which will be unconventional and they have to defend the statements with proper arguments.

Rules

  • Vulgar content is to be avoided strictly.
  • Use of foul language prohibited
  • Decision of judges is final
  • Both Hindi and English are allowed
  • Questions with star mark will be given extra points if answered
